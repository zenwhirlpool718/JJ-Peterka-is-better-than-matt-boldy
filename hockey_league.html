Rules for Minecraft Ice Hockey

Games are played 2v2 in 3 two minute periods

Each player gets a goal stick and a shooting stick

Only one player can occupy the crease at a time


Time Stimes when the following happens:

  1. A goal is scored
  2. A penalty occures
  3. The puck is played out of bounds
  4. The puck is set on fire

Time starts again when the puck is spawned

Penalties

Major: 15 seconds

  1. Intentionally hitting a player once
  2. Accidentally hitting a player 3rd offence
  3. Roughing (a player hits anotehr after the play has stopped or multiple times during play)

Double Minor: 10 seconds

  1. Accidentally hitting a player 2nd offence
  2. Using the goal stick out of the crease intentionally

Minor: 5 seconds

  1. Using goal stick out of crease
  2. fighting (both player had to have hit each other)
